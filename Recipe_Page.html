Homemade Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced pepperoni or toppings of your choice (e.g., mushrooms, bell peppers, onions)
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Olive oil, for drizzling

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone that has been lightly dusted with cornmeal or flour.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add your chosen toppings evenly over the cheese.
  7. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese, dried oregano, and dried basil over the toppings.
  8. Drizzle a little olive oil over the pizza.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and lightly browned.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
